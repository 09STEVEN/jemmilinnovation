About Jemmil Innovations

Your trusted partner for quality business supplies

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Jemmil Innovations Ltd began as a small supplier of cleaning chemicals to local restaurants in Lagos. Recognizing the need for reliable, high-quality supplies in the hospitality and retail sectors, we expanded our product range and services to meet growing demand.

Today, we serve businesses across Nigeria, providing comprehensive solutions that help our clients maintain the highest standards of cleanliness, efficiency, and presentation.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, high-quality products and solutions that help businesses operate more efficiently while maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and customer satisfaction.
